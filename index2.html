CAST

Ian Somerhalder

Damon Salvatore

Damon Salvatore is the former antagonist. He is a 145 year old cured vampire and the oldest son of Giuseppe and Lilian Salvatore. He is portrayed as a charming, handsome and snide person who loves tricking humans, takes pleasure in feeding and killing them. Damon and Stefan both fall in love with Katherine Pierce and later with Elena Gilbert too, causing intense rivalry. They both resolve their issues and rekindle their brotherhood. Later, he becomes more kind, compassionate and develops new friendships. Damon and Elena get married and live happily.



Paul Wesley

Stefan Salvatore

Stefan Salvatore is the male protagonist. He is a 145 year old cured vampire and a doppelgänger of Silas. Stefan is the youngest child of Giuseppe Salvatore and Lillian Salvatore. He has an older brother, Damon with whom he has a strained relationship with, stemming from the appearance of Katherine Pierce. Stefan's life has been fraught with constant change and various complications. Throughout the series, outside forces threaten those he loved and what he most desired: a normal, human life.



Nina Dobrev

Elena Gilbert

Elena Gilbert is the former female protagonist. At the beginning, she appears to be a regular human, but is then revealed to be the last Petrova doppelgänger. She becomes a vampire despite never wanting to be one but later is cured. She is sporty, smart, compassionate, empathetic, caring and friendly. She could be really vulnerable at times. Her actions and beliefs have been most influenced by her on-and-off relationships with the Salvatore brothers. Damon and Elena get married and live happily.



Steven R. Mcqueen

Jeremy Gilbert

Jeremy is the adopted brother and biological cousin of Elena Gilbert, as well as a former medium, although this status became defunct following the collapse of the Other Side. He is a member of the Brotherhood of the Five. His best friends are Matt Donovan, Tyler Lockwood, and Bonnie Bennett, who was also his girlfriend. Later, Jeremy moves on with his life and leaves Mystic Falls. He comes back after Elena Gilbert awakens from her magical slumber. He now works as a teacher at Alaric's Salvatore Boarding School.



Mathew Davis

Alaric K. Saltzman

Alaric attended Duke University and studies the supernatural. Alaric is a kind person, willing to help others. He is friendly and understanding, but is serious when the time calls for it. He is also very curious, when he was quizzing students on Mr. Tanner's death. Later, Alaric and Jo get married and are expecting a baby. Jo is killed at their wedding and Caroline gives birth to their twins, Josie and Lizzie. Alaric opens a school for gifted youngsters, where he and his daughters are watched over by Jo.



Candice King

Caroline Forbes

Caroline is the daughter of William Forbes and Sheriff Elizabeth Forbes. She is also the surrogate mother of Alaric and Jo's twin daughters, Lizzie and Josie. Prior to her transition into a vampire, she had insecurities, which caused her to be very competitive. She has always had a strong bond with her childhood friends,Elena and Bonnie. She is a member of the Salvatore Family, due to her brief marriage to Stefan. Later, Caroline stops looking at her life in a shallow and negative light and her insecurities fade.



Joseph Morgan

Klaus Mikaelson

Klaus, his step-father and half-siblings become the very first vampires after their mother, a witch, cast a spell on them in order to protect them from werewolves. It is later found out that Klaus' biological father is a werewolf, which makes him the only original vampire-werewolf hybrid in existence. However, to release his werewolf powers, a Petrova doppelgänger has to be sacrificed. Klaus finds Elena Gilbert, for whom he arrives at Mystic Falls. Klaus falls in love with the vampire Caroline Forbes.



Daniel Gillies

Elijah Mikaelson

Elijah is an original vampire, who undaggers all his siblings who were in coffins. After he foils his mother's plans to kill all the siblings, he leaves Mystic Falls, ashamed of his and his family's own barbaric actions. He is shown to be one of the most sophisticated and peaceful of the Original siblings. Elena and Elijah become fond of each other's company and make a deal. He takes Klaus and his siblings with him and leaves town, and in exchange, he makes sure that Klaus will not bother Elena and her friends.



Kat Graham

Bonnie Bennet

Bonnie is good friends with Elena and Caroline. She discovers that she is born from a line of witches. Her grandmother, Sheila, helps her learn to use her powers. Bonnie continues training and becomes powerful. She and Enzo develop a romantic relationship. Later, her grief from his death causes her to unlock her psychic abilities. Inspired by her ancestors and Enzo, Bonnie gains new empowerment and saves Mystic Falls. She lives her life to the fullest and leaves to travel the world as he watches over her.



Michael Trevino

Tyler Lockwood

Tyler Lockwood is the son of Mayor Richard and Carol Lockwood. He is best friends with Matt Donovan and is dating Vicki. He has a short temper and learns from his uncle, Mason, that he acts this way due to the werewolf gene. After he triggers the werewolf curse by killing, he laves town to protect everyone. However, he returns when his mother loses her life and is then turned into a hybrid by Klaus. Tyler is later killed by Damon. He reunites with Vicki, where the two find peace together and continue to watch over Matt.



Zach Roerig

Matt Donovan

Matt is born and raised in the town of Mystic Falls. He is the son of Kelly Donovan and Peter Maxwell. After the death of Elena's parents, she breaks up with Matt because she loses interest in him. Matt still cares about Elena deeply and they maintain a good friendship. Matt struggles with his sister, Vicki, because she is a drug user and then she tragically dies. Matt becomes sheriff and is about to run for mayor, where he's watched over by Vicki and Tyler. He is the only character who remains a human throughout the series.



Claire Holt

Rebekah Mikaelson

Rebekah is the second daughter of Mikael and Esther. Throughout her millennium-long life as an original, Rebekah's personality and actions have been heavily influenced by her relationship with Klaus and her desire to live a normal, human life. She fled New Orleans with Klaus to escape her father Mikael where she was briefly in a relationship with Stefan. She had several flings with both Stefan and Damon before finding herself in a romantic relationship with a human, Matt Donovan.
